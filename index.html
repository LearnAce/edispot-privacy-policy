Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 31, 2025

1. Introduction

Edispot is an educational app designed for TNPSC exam preparation. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

2. Data We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email (if signed in with Google).
  • Device Information: Device ID, OS version, crash logs, etc.
  • Usage Data: In-app activity, viewed content, and feature usage.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide access to TNPSC preparation content.
  • To personalize user experience and improve app features.
  • To send push notifications (with user consent).
  • To process purchases via Razorpay or Google Play Billing.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services like Firebase, Google Play Billing, Razorpay, and AdMob. These may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4A. In-App Purchases and Payments

Our app offers in-app purchases for premium features priced at ₹99 and ₹149 (plus applicable GST). Payments are securely processed via Google Play Billing or Razorpay.

We do not store or have access to your credit/debit card or UPI information. All transactions and billing data are handled securely by these payment providers, in accordance with their privacy policies and Google Play’s terms.

5. Ads & Analytics

We use Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics to show ads and analyze app performance. These services may collect anonymized data.

6. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for users above the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
